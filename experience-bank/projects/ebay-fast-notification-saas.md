# eBay Fast Notification SaaS

Built a SaaS application that gave users fast notifications for relevant eBay items, helping them discover listings quickly enough to be among the first potential buyers. The product was beta tested by ETB Technologies, a hardware company in Scotland.

## What It Did

- Monitored eBay listings for relevant items.
- Sent fast notifications when matching items appeared.
- Helped ETB Technologies react quickly to buying opportunities.
- Reached beta testing with an external hardware company in Scotland.

## CV Bullet Raw Material

- Built a marketplace-monitoring SaaS product that delivered fast notifications for relevant eBay listings.
- Took a SaaS product to external beta testing with ETB Technologies, a Scottish hardware company.
- Developed alerting workflows to help users react quickly to time-sensitive purchasing opportunities.
