# MCP-Powered Asset Management News Aggregation Service

Built a news aggregation tool initially for the CEO of a large asset manager, described publicly as managing roughly £171.8bn in AUM as of 31 March 2026. Used MCP servers to enhance the tool for investment-team workflows.

## What It Did

- Aggregated RSS feeds for asset-management/investment stakeholders.
- Classified articles into nine categories.
- Built a frontend to search and filter articles.
- Built an MCP layer on top of the aggregation system.
- Used `SKILL.md` files to describe what different user types would find interesting.
- Enabled an LLM to fetch relevant news articles based on user-interest profiles.
- Supported SaaS applications intended for investment teams or asset managers.
- Required learning and applying domain knowledge about asset-management teams, including product specialists, product managers, investment teams, and sales/client-facing workflows.

## CV Bullet Raw Material

- Built an MCP-powered news aggregation service for senior stakeholders at a roughly £171.8bn AUM asset manager, supporting investment research and market-monitoring workflows.
- Aggregated RSS feeds, classified articles into nine categories, and built a frontend for searching and filtering market news.
- Used MCP and `SKILL.md` user-interest profiles to let an LLM retrieve relevant articles for different asset-management personas.
- Developed LLM/MCP-enhanced SaaS tools for investment-team and asset-management use cases.
- Worked directly with senior asset-management stakeholders to shape client-facing SaaS product requirements.
