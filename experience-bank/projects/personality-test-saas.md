# Asset Management Personality-Test SaaS

Built the software for an external-facing SaaS product in the asset-management space. The product is currently being taken to market, with around 50 C-suite executives in asset management having completed the 200-question test. The assessment/test itself was designed by others; ownership here is software delivery, demos, maintenance, and iteration.

## What It Did

- Delivered a personality-test SaaS experience for senior asset-management stakeholders.
- Supported roughly 50 C-suite executives completing a 200-question test.
- Built the software platform; did not design the assessment itself.
- Involved meetings with C-suite individuals to gather feedback for product iteration.
- Required end-to-end development ownership, including implementation, demos, maintenance, and iteration.
