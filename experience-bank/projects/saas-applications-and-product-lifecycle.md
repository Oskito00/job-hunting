# SaaS Applications and Product Lifecycle Ownership

Built end-to-end SaaS applications and helped shape them through requirements gathering, client feedback, beta testing, and product iteration.

## What It Did

- Built Flask and Express APIs that exposed product logic to frontend interfaces.
- Built one external-facing SaaS product being taken to market in the asset-management space.
- Supported internal production recruitment tools alongside the external-facing SaaS product.
- Used GitHub/version control to document releases.
- Worked with recruiters to understand requirements, inefficiencies, and operational needs.
- Identified process inefficiencies in recruiter workflows and translated them into product changes.
- Presented and launched internal tools to recruiters, explaining workflows and gathering rollout feedback.
- Converted qualitative and quantitative feedback into product improvements.
- Supported beta testing and identified potential beta users.
- Built an outreach tracking system to manage who had been contacted and when follow-up was needed.
- Participated in client interviews and fed feedback into new requirements.
- Participated in meetings with C-suite asset-management stakeholders to gather feedback for product iteration.
- Had awareness of business service costs and expenses, possibly P&L-related responsibilities.
